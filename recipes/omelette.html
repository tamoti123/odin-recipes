Ready Omelette

Rustle up this omelette for your little one. Suitable from 6 months, it's a great first food to get weaning children familiar with new tastes and textures

Ingredients

  • 1 knob of butter
  • 1 tomato deseeded and diced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 garlic clove crushed
  • 2-3 eggs
  • approx 30g grated cheddar cheese

Steps to prepare/method

Step 1
  1. Melt half of the butter in a frying pan then add the tomato and oregano,
  2. While cooking it for 2-3 minutes
  3. Remove to a plate and wipe the frying pan clean.

Step 2

  1. Whisk the eggs together,
  2. melt the remaining butter in the frying pan then add the eggs.

Step 3

  1. Cook on a low heat,
  2. using a spatula to lift the sides up and allow the raw egg to escape out the sides (this gives you a fluffier omelette).

Step 4

  1. After 2 minutes place the tomato and cheese across half of the omelette.
  2. Cook for a further minute then remove from the heat.
  3. Flip the free half of the omelette over the tomato and cheese half.
  4. Cut into fingers for baby to serve themselves.
